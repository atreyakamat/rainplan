# **1. Executive Summary**
78 E Loka is an all-India local experience platform designed for tourists who want to discover the real India — not the packaged, tourist-trap version. The app connects travelers directly with locals offering authentic food, cultural, and adventure experiences, while using intelligent weather-aware technology to surface the right experience at exactly the right moment.

Starting in Goa and expanding to Northeast India and Kashmir, 78 E Loka aims to become the definitive platform for authentic travel experiences across India.

## **Mission**
To empower local communities by connecting them with travelers seeking genuine, unforgettable experiences — while using intelligent technology to make discovery effortless, timely, and deeply personal.

# **3. Problem Statement**
Tourists visiting India face three recurring frustrations:

- Existing platforms (Zomato, MakeMyTrip, Airbnb) show the same generic, commercialized experiences everywhere.
- There is no intelligent system that adapts recommendations based on real-time conditions like weather, time of day, or duration of stay.
- Local hosts — fishermen, home cooks, artisans, adventurers — have no platform to monetize their unique knowledge and skills.

78 E Loka solves all three problems in one intelligent, beautifully designed app.

# **6. Core Service Architecture**

# **7. Weather Intelligence Engine**
Weather intelligence is not a section of the app — it runs silently in the background and influences everything the user sees. The app uses real-time weather data (similar to Windy/Nimbus) combined with AI logic to surface the most relevant experiences at any given moment.

|**Weather Condition**|**What App Surfaces First**|**What App Hides**|
| :- | :- | :- |
|Sunny Morning|Fishing trips, heritage walks, outdoor markets, waterfall hikes|Indoor-only experiences|
|Hot Afternoon|Dive bars, indoor craft classes, cool hidden caves|Strenuous outdoor activities|
|Raining|Home cooked meals, pottery classes, dive bars, cave visits|Outdoor adventures, beach activities|
|Stormy / Heavy Rain|Indoor experiences only + safety alert to user|All outdoor experiences|
|Perfect Golden Evening|Sunset hidden spots, local bars, night markets|Full-day activities|
|Early Morning|Sunrise breakfast, fishing trips, morning markets|Evening/night experiences|

The weather engine also provides detailed meteorological data — rainfall radar, storm intensity, humidity levels, wind speed — so tourists can plan hours in advance, not just react in the moment.

# **8. Miss Your Flight Feature**
This is not a standalone section. It is a smart filter embedded within all three pillars. When a user activates it, the app filters all available experiences by proximity to the nearest airport and sorts them by duration — showing only what is realistically possible within the user's available time window.

|<p>**How It Works**</p><p>User inputs: 'I have 3 hours near Dabolim Airport'</p><p>App filters: All experiences within 30-minute radius of airport</p><p>App sorts: By duration — shortest first, with travel time included</p><p>Result: A clean list of 3-5 realistic options with booking in one tap</p>|
| :- |

# **9. Host Onboarding & Management**
## **Host Sign Up Flow**
- Local submits basic profile — name, location, type of experience, photos
- 78 E Loka team verifies the host within 48 hours
- Host gets a simple dashboard to manage bookings, availability, and earnings
- All payments processed through the app — no cash, no direct transfers

## **Host Pricing & Commission**

|**Phase**|**Listing Fee**|**Commission**|**Rationale**|
| :- | :- | :- | :- |
|Launch Phase|Free|8-12% per booking|Attract maximum hosts early|
|Growth Phase|Minimal monthly fee (TBD)|8-12% per booking|Sustainable platform economics|
|Scale Phase|Tiered based on host level|Reduced commission for top hosts|Reward loyalty|

|**Pillar**|**Commission Rate**|
| :- | :- |
|Taste (food & drink)|8%|
|Live — Calm Experiences|10%|
|Live — Adventure|12%|
|Discover (guided hidden spot experiences)|10%|

## **Host Retention Strategy**
- SuperHost badge system — New Host → Trusted Host → SuperHost
- SuperHosts get top placement in search, homepage features, and lower commission
- 78 E Loka provides free professional photography for top hosts
- Monthly earnings report and tax-ready invoicing provided through app
- Reviews are tied to the platform — hosts cannot take their reputation elsewhere

- All payments processed exclusively through the app — no cash, no off-platform transfers
- Payment gateway: Razorpay (supports UPI, cards, net banking, wallets)
- Foreign tourist support: International cards, multi-currency
- Payouts to hosts: Automated, weekly, directly to bank account
